About Aria

Where Luxury
Meets Lifestyle

Aria Apartments is Columbia's premier luxury residential community, designed for those who refuse to compromise. Nestled in the scenic Discovery Park neighborhood, our resort-style community blends sophisticated design with unparalleled amenities.

Set beside a stunning 40-acre lake and nature trail system, Aria is more than an apartment — it's a lifestyle. Whether you're a young professional, graduate student, or growing family, Aria offers the perfect home base in the heart of Columbia, Missouri.

  • Built 2016 • Professionally managed luxury community
  • Minutes from University of Missouri
  • Surrounded by parks, trails & great dining
  • Studio, 1BR, 2BR & 3BR apartments available
